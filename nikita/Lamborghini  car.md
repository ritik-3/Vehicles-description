Lamborghini is an iconic Italian brand known for luxury super sports cars, founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, famous for models like the Miura, Countach, Aventador, and modern hybrids like the Revuelto, Urus SUV, and Temerario, blending powerful engines with advanced aerodynamics and distinctive design, all while moving towards electrification through their "Direzione Cor Tauri" program.

Key Models:-

Revuelto: Lamborghini's first High Performance Electrified Vehicle (HPEV), a V12 hybrid supercar replacing the Aventador, featuring advanced aerodynamics and hybrid tech.
Urus: A Super SUV, with the new Urus SE being a plug-in hybrid, offering versatility for various terrains.
Temerario: A new hybrid model designed as a successor to the Huracan, featuring powerful V8 turbo-hybrid performance.
Huracan: A popular V10-powered sports car, known for its performance and available in various trims like the Evo and STO.
