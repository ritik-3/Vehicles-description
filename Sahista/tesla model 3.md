# Tesla Model 3
The Tesla Model 3 is an all-electric compact sedan.
It offers long-range battery options and quick acceleration.
The minimalist interior centers around a large touchscreen display.
Model 3 includes advanced driver-assistance features from Tesla.
Over-the-air updates add features and improve performance.
Charging can be done at home or Supercharger stations.
Regenerative braking helps extend driving range.
The Performance variant delivers sports-car level acceleration.
Standard models prioritize efficiency and daily usability.
Safety ratings are among the best in its class.
The electric powertrain requires less routine maintenance.
Autopilot provides lane-keeping and adaptive cruise control.
Customization includes wheel, paint, and interior options.
Cabin space is roomy for a compact sedan segment.
Model 3 competes strongly on price and total cost of ownership.
Software controls many vehicle functions and personalization.
The car's design emphasizes aerodynamic efficiency.
Buyers benefit from lower emissions and quieter driving.
The Model 3 helped accelerate mainstream electric vehicle adoption.
